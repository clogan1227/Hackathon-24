Importance of Financial literacy

Financial literacy is crucial for navigating the complexities of the modern financial world. It empowers individuals to make informed decisions about money, budgeting, saving, investing, and managing debt. With financial literacy, people can better understand financial products and services, such as loans, credit cards, and mortgages, and make wise choices that align with their financial goals. Additionally, financial literacy helps individuals protect themselves from fraud and predatory financial practices. By teaching financial literacy, we can empower individuals to build a secure financial future and contribute to economic stability and growth.
