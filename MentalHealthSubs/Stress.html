Stress

Stress is like carrying a heavy load on your shoulders, weighing you down and making every day feel like a struggle. It's that constant feeling of pressure and tension, as if you're always on the edge of a breaking point. Just as anxiety can make you feel overwhelmed by worry, stress can make you feel overwhelmed by the demands of life. It can affect you both physically and emotionally, causing headaches, muscle tension, and sleep problems. Stress can also impact your mood, making you feel irritable, anxious, or even depressed. Like anxiety, stress is a natural response to challenges, but it's important to find healthy ways to manage it to avoid long-term negative effects on your health and well-being.
