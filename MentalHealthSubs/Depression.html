Depression

Depression is a mental health condition that can feel like being stuck in a deep, dark hole. It's like walking through thick fog that clouds your thoughts and makes everything seem overwhelming. Just as anxiety can make you feel on edge and worried, depression can make you feel empty and hopeless. It can drain your energy and motivation, making even simple tasks feel like a huge burden. Depression is not just feeling sad; it's a persistent feeling of sadness that lingers and affects your daily life. Like anxiety, depression is a common mental health issue, and it's important to seek support and treatment to help navigate through it.
