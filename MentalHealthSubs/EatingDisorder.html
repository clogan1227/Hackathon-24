Eating Disorders

Having an eating disorder is like being trapped in a complicated and often overwhelming relationship with food, body image, and emotions. It can feel like a constant battle between wanting to control your eating habits and feeling controlled by them. Just as anxiety can make you feel trapped by irrational fears, an eating disorder can make you feel trapped by unhealthy thoughts and behaviors surrounding food and weight. It can lead to extreme behaviors like restricting food intake, binge eating, or purging, all of which can have serious physical and emotional consequences. Eating disorders can also cause feelings of guilt, shame, and worthlessness, making it difficult to seek help. Like anxiety, eating disorders are complex and require compassionate support and professional treatment to overcome.
